## [speeds and feeds](http://new.industrialpress.com/machineryhandbook)
- chip load: ~ 0.001-0.010"
- feed rate (inches per minute) / (RPM x number of flutes) 
- cut depth: ~ tool diameter
- step-over: ~ tool diameter/2
